How to Delete Your Account

We respect your right to control your personal data. This guide explains how to delete your account and what happens to your data when you do so.

1. Disconnecting from Places

Before deleting your account, you may want to disconnect from individual places:

  1. Open the app and go to "Connected Places"
  2. Select the place you wish to disconnect from
  3. Tap "Disconnect"
  4. You will see a warning: "Are you sure you want to disconnect this connection? All your reports will be deleted from this place!"
  5. If you're sure, confirm the disconnection
Important: When you disconnect from a place, all reports you've made for that place will be deleted from our system. However, please note that any saved or downloaded copies of these reports by third parties may still exist and are beyond our ability to delete.

2. Deleting Your Entire Account

If you wish to completely delete your account:

  1. Open the app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll down and tap on "Delete Account"
  4. You will see a warning: "All data of the current account will be permanently deleted. Are you sure you want to continue?"
  5. If you're certain, confirm the deletion

What Happens When You Delete Your Account:

  • All your personal information will be permanently deleted from our servers
  • Your user profile will be removed
  • All reports you've made across all places will be deleted from our system
  • Your account will be deactivated and you will no longer be able to log in
Please Note:
  • This process is irreversible. Once your account is deleted, it cannot be restored.
  • As with disconnecting from individual places, we cannot delete copies of your reports that may have been saved or downloaded by third parties.
  • If you're a Special Agent or have any ongoing contractual obligations with us, please contact our support team before deleting your account to ensure proper closure of your professional relationship.

Need Help?

If you have any questions about the account deletion process or need assistance, please contact our support team at postmaster@getboby.com.

We're sorry to see you go, but we respect your decision. Thank you for being part of our community.
